About Our Project

This website was built as part of the 4COSC011W Web Design and Development module at the University of Westminster. Our topic, Life Under Water (SDG 14), focuses on raising awareness of the importance of conserving and sustainably using Earth's oceans and marine resources. Each team member contributed unique pages and interactive features to create a cohesive, accessible, and visually engaging website.
